Campaign (Marketing)

Tables:
- Roadmaps

- Projects
- Campaigns
- Content
- Tasks
- Creative Requests
- Sprints

Business Rules:
- Every Task belongs to one Project.
- Campaigns are Marketing only.
- Sprints are Product only.
- Content belongs to a Project.
- Projects belong to one Roadmap.
